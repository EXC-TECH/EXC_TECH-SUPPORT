I Can't See The Camera Feed

Current Step:
  1. Check camera cable
  2. Check connection
  3. Locating the camera feed

If you are connected to your MakerBot Replicator and still cannot view the camera feed, or if you are receiving an error message, the cable that connects to the camera may have become loose in shipping. Locate the small gray cable plugged in behind the LCD screen that connects to the camera. If the cable is not plugged in, please attach it. If it is already attached, unplug it and plug it back in.
